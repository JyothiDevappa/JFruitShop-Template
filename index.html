Our Story

It all started one hot summer afternoon when Joo realized everyone was craving something cold and fruity — just like her. That’s when the idea of Joo’s Juice Shop popped into her head. Not a real shop, of course — just an online dream filled with smoothies, colors, and summer vibes. What began as a silly thought soon became her favorite way to imagine a world that always smells like fresh mangoes.
